# Quality Rubric (Academic Rigor)

This rubric defines the minimum bar for considering an artifact “usable” and for promoting it toward “stable”.

## Universal checks (all canonical artifacts)

- Correct layer placement (belief/decision/experiment/synthesis/resource).
- `## Metadata` present with at least Status + Created.
- Key terms are defined, not implied.
- Claims are scoped (what is included/excluded).
- External dependencies are cited in `## Sources`.
- Links connect the artifact into the graph (at least one meaningful backlink or forward link).

## Beliefs (`beliefs/`)

Minimum bar:

- Clear `## Statement` (single-sentence version included).
- Explicit `## Assumptions`.
- `## Evidence` includes sources and strength/limits.
- `## Counterarguments` includes credible alternatives and failure modes.
- `## Status` is justified.
- `## Revision history` exists (even if initially “created”).

## Decisions (`decisions/`)

Minimum bar:

- Clear `## Decision question` and `## Decision` (what is chosen).
- `## Context` snapshot (constraints, time horizon).
- `## Alternatives considered` (at least 2 when plausible).
- `## Expected outcomes` with measurable signals.
- `## Reconsideration triggers` (when to revisit).
- Links to supporting beliefs and related experiments.

## Experiments (`experiments/`)

Minimum bar:

- `## Hypothesis` (what you expect and why).
- `## Setup` and `## Procedure` (reproducible enough for future-you).
- `## Measures` (what is measured, how).
- `## Results` (data/observations).
- `## Interpretation` (what changed in belief/decision terms).
- Links to the decision tested and beliefs stressed.

## Synthesis (`synthesis/`)

Minimum bar:

- Scope defined (time window or topic).
- Inputs listed (links to key beliefs/decisions/experiments).
- Extracted patterns and updated constraints.
- Explicit list of belief updates (with links).
- Open questions recorded.

## Resources (`resources/`)

Minimum bar:

- Proper citation in `## Reference`.
- Summary that distinguishes source claims vs your interpretations.
- Extracted claims (candidate beliefs) and key terms.
